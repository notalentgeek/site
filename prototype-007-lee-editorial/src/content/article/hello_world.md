There is something satisfying about a personal website that is just text. No hero images competing for your attention. No animated gradients. No "above the fold" anxiety. Just words, set in a typeface you trust, against a background that gets out of the way.

This site is built on that conviction. The column is narrow on purpose — roughly 580 pixels, the sweet spot where your eye can track from line to line without getting lost. The font is a serif because serifs were designed for exactly this: extended reading.

## Why bother?

Social platforms come and go. A personal domain is the one URL you fully control. Write here, link from everywhere else.

## What you will find

I write about web development, tools I use, and things I am learning. Occasionally I record videos. Sometimes I just collect links to things I found interesting.

This short film follows the 14-month construction of the Boundary Pavilion in Yoshino, Nara Prefecture. The structure uses locally sourced Yoshino cedar throughout — a material tradition dating back over 500 years in this region.

Key moments documented:
- Site clearing and foundation work in winter conditions
- Timber frame raising by a crew of six
- Installation of the cedar screen system
- First light through the completed screens at dawn

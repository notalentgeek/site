There is something deeply satisfying about a blank page. Not the anxiety kind — the possibility kind. Every project begins here, at zero, with nothing but intention.

This site is intentionally sparse. No hero images. No gradients. No animations competing for attention. Just text, links, and generous whitespace.

## Why minimal

Complexity is easy. Restraint is hard. The goal is not to strip away features until nothing is left — it is to strip away everything that does not serve the reader.

A good interface disappears. You do not notice the typography, the spacing, the color. You just read, and it feels right.

## What to expect

Writing about interfaces, tools, and the small details that make software feel considered. Occasional links to things worth reading. Maybe some videos.

Nothing more than that.
